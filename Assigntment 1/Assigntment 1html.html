Song characteristics

  • The song was written by Lipa, MNEK, Larzz Principato, Skyler Stonestreet, and Whiskey Water, with Koz handling the production
  • The song is about an ex-boyfriend who intends to get back with the singer, who is uninterested.
  • Commercially, the song reached the top 10 of charts in 20 territories worldwide, including Croatia and Ireland where it reached the summit.
  • The song was recorded at Paramount Studios in Los Angeles
